Version: 2.0.0
Date: 2024-11-11
  Info:
    - Re-release for Factorio 2.0
  Changes:
    - Battery recycling is disabled by default for new games to avoid problems with Space Age.
    - Battery-electric locomotives are now placed in the same item group as the other train items.
    - Renamed basic batteries and chargers to have a "1" at the end.
  Features:
    - Battery pack charging efficiency can be adjusted. This will influence charging time and thus total power consumption.
    - The capacity of battery packs can be adjusted if you don't want to wait for the high-capacity battery packs late in the game or want more capacity in general.
